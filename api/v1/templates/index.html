About this project

Our team is made up of people with different backgrounds and experiences, from working in clinics to working in restaurants, and do you know what we had in common when we worked there? We had to be attentive to the metrological measurements in the processes.
I mean that we should have a lot of control of the temperatures in the refrigerators or the environment, the atmospheric pressures or within a gas network, so continuous records of these measurements were necessary, records that are not always taken in time for different reasons.
That is why we developed a solution that allows us to measure different magnitudes as required by the user and deliver them to them in an easy-to-use web app so that they have knowledge of the measurements in their process and can take actions when they are not expected.
